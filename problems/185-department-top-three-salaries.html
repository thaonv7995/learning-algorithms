Department Top Three Salaries

O(n log n) O(n) 60.5% AC
Mô tả
Giống LC184 nhưng trả mọi nhân viên thuộc top 3 mức lương cao nhất trong mỗi department (tie vẫn tính).
Ví dụ
Ví dụ 1
InIT salaries 85000,90000,90000,70000
OutIT: 90000×2, 85000
Ví dụ 2
InSales one employee
Outthat employee
Ý tưởng
DENSE_RANK() OVER (PARTITION BY departmentId ORDER BY salary DESC) AS rk WHERE rk <= 3.
Mã nguồn
solution.c C99 
/* SQL */
SELECT d.name AS Department, e.name AS Employee, e.salary AS Salary
FROM (
  SELECT *, DENSE_RANK() OVER (PARTITION BY departmentId ORDER BY salary DESC) AS rk
  FROM Employee
) e
JOIN Department d ON e.departmentId = d.id
WHERE e.rk <= 3;
